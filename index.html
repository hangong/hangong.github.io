Dr Han Gong is a camera ISP algorithm engineer at Apple (Cambridge, UK). He invented, implemented and shipped algorithms for Apple camera colour optimisation. He was a Lecturer at the University of East Anglia (UEA, 2018-2020), a post-doc at UEA (2015-2018) and Bath (2015) and a visiting researcher at Edinburgh (2016-2018) and Cambridge (2015-2018).

He has a PhD in Computer Vision (Bath). He no longer publishes papers but focuses solely on Apple product shipping. See also his GitHub, LinkedIn and Google Scholar.
